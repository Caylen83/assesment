An Introduction To Me

I currently work as a customer service supervisor for the sales department of an engineering firm. I use a PC daily along with an internet phone system to process orders and answer emails etc. As half our business is heat ex-changers/oil coolers we often use a program that was developed for us to calculate the heat load of a hydraulic system by inputting relevant parameters, then it basically spits out a part number so we can quote the customer. Our main business system is called Pronto which is OK but kind of clunky and has recently been moved to online only instead of program that has been installed. Even in our small company there is a huge amount going on behind the scenes in regards to networking, VPN connections and the way that everything needs to work in harmony so that we can all do our job efficiently, especially with people working from home nowadays.

Why I'm Inetersted In IT

Personally, I enjoy that kind of more complex connectivity of things behind the scenes and the reason I am getting into this course is to hopefully have a much broader understanding of computer systems in general along with networking and maybe coding could be useful. I was introduced to computers when I was quite young when my father gave me an IBM 286, giant floppy disc DOS machine and apparently, he had a job once where he was transferring data by moving giant cartridges or something haha not sure but it's amazing how far the industry has come. Having said that I would like to get up to speed and move forward along with it, Ideally, I would have a solely IT based career in the future.
